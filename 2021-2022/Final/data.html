Data and Information

A CSA might not be responsible for getting the data, but they’re certainly responsible for modeling it and keeping it safe, from any kind of prying eyes. Because data is such a big deal for companies, there are always jobs that revolve around it. This is where CSAs come in.

A CSA might be responsible for diagnosing database and computer systems, or again, protecting it from unwanted usage. You might even take a course in data science, but end up working as a CSA. They’re just that similar, and overlap is, and can be, common.

And finally, as previously stated, CSAs might use a variety of techniques to use, store, and present data. These techniques may range to data modeling and designing more minor computer systems.
