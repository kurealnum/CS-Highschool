Cybersecurity and Computer System Analysts

A Computer Systems Analyst (CSA) is a very similar profession to Cybersecurity. This is because both professions try to evaluate weaknesses in a system, and change that. They also do some reporting on what they changed, and what needs to be changed.

A Computer Systems Analyst (CSA) is a very similar profession to Cybersecurity. This is because both professions try to evaluate weaknesses in a system, and change that. They also do some reporting on what they changed, and what needs to be changed.

Finally, they also share similarities in their style of consultation. They might need to communicate an error or bug to their higher–ups, so they’ll both need good communication skills. This might come in the form of a presentation, phone call, or just a simple email.
